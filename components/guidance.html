Introduction to Kundalini Tantra

Discover the ancient science of awakening dormant spiritual energy. Learn about the subtle body, energy channels, and the sacred path of transformation.

The Seven Chakras
  • Muladhara (Root Chakra)
  • Svadhisthana (Sacral Chakra)
  • Manipura (Solar Plexus Chakra)
  • Anahata (Heart Chakra)
  • Vishuddha (Throat Chakra)
  • Ajna (Third Eye Chakra)
  • Sahasrara (Crown Chakra)

Preparation & Lifestyle

Create a supportive foundation for your practice through daily routines, mindful nutrition, and environmental preparation.

Morning Sadhana
  1. Wake before sunrise, hydrate with warm water
  2. 5-10 min breathing practice (Pranayama)
  3. 10-15 min gentle asana sequence
  4. 10 min seated meditation
Pre-Practice Checklist
  • Empty or light stomach
  • Quiet, clean space prepared
  • Phone on silent, journal ready
  • Clear intention set

Safety & Warnings

Essential guidelines to ensure a safe and grounded practice. Know when to pause, seek guidance, or consult professionals.

Do's
  • Proceed slowly and mindfully
  • Practice regular grounding exercises
  • Keep a detailed practice journal
  • Rest when your body asks
Don'ts
  • Force intense practices prematurely
  • Ignore prolonged physical or emotional distress
  • Practice without proper guidance initially
  • Skip integration and grounding
